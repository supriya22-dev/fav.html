My Favourite People

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam (Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam) was a renowned Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India, serving from 2002 to 2007. Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he came from a humble background, the youngest child in a family with limited means. Despite financial challenges, Kalam excelled academically, earning a degree in physics from St. Joseph’s College and later studying aeronautical engineering at the Madras Institute of Technology.

Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi, known as the "Father of the Nation" in India, was a pivotal leader in the country’s struggle for independence from British rule. Born as Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi on 2nd October 1869 in Porbandar, Gujarat, he grew up in a Hindu family and later went to England to study law. After completing his studies, Gandhi practiced law in South Africa, where he first experienced racial discrimination. This injustice led him to become a political activist and develop his philosophy of Satyagraha, or nonviolent resistance, which would become central to his life’s work.

Mukesh Ambani

Mukesh Ambani, born on April 19, 1957, in Aden (then a British colony, now Yemen), is an Indian business magnate and the chairman and managing director of Reliance Industries Limited (RIL), one of India's largest and most diversified conglomerates. As of May 2025, he is the richest person in Asia and ranks 13th globally, with a net worth estimated at $108 billion. Ambani has consistently featured among the world’s wealthiest individuals, topping Forbes’ list of India’s richest for many years.




Sundar pichai

Sundar Pichai, born Pichai Sundararajan on June 10, 1972, in Madras (now Chennai), India, is a renowned Indian-American business executive and the CEO of both Alphabet Inc. and Google LLC. Pichai’s rise from humble beginnings in a modest two-room apartment in Chennai to the helm of one of the world’s most influential technology companies is widely recognized as a story of perseverance, intellect, and visionary leadership
